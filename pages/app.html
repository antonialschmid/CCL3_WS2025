Summary App Usability Reflection

App Overview

App Screens

The following screenshots show the main screens of the Gratitude Garden app and illustrate the core user flow from reflection to review.

Garden

The Garden screen is the emotional center of the app. A single plant visualizes the user’s gratitude journey and grows over time.

Add Entry

Users can add a new gratitude entry by selecting a date, writing a short reflection, and choosing a mood.

History

The History screen provides a calendar-based overview of past entries and supports reflection over time.

Edit Entry

Existing entries can be reviewed and edited, allowing users to adjust text, mood, or date.

Settings

The Settings screen provides access to app preferences and additional information.
