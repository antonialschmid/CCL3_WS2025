Gratitude Garden

Creative Coding Lab 3 · Winter Semester 2025

Antonia Schmid · Kathrin Buchberger

Project Summary

Gratitude Garden is a wellbeing-focused Android application designed to support gratitude journaling in a calm and low-pressure way. Instead of numbers, streaks, or productivity metrics, the app visualises emotional reflection through the growth of a single plant.

Each gratitude entry contributes to the plant’s development. Over time, the plant progresses through predefined growth stages, serving as a visual metaphor for personal reflection and emotional awareness.

Key Features

  • Daily gratitude entries with mood selection
  • Calendar-based overview of past entries
  • Visual plant growth instead of numerical progress
  • Offline-first design using a local SQLite database
