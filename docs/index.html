Summary App Usability Reflection

Gratitude Garden

Creative Coding Lab 3 – Winter Semester 2025
Antonia Schmid · Kathrin Buchberger

Project Summary

Gratitude Garden is a wellbeing-focused Android application that explores gratitude journaling in a calm and non-competitive way. Instead of using streaks, statistics, or performance metrics, the app visualises emotional reflection through the gradual growth of a single plant.

Concept

The core concept of Gratitude Garden is to represent gratitude as a slow, organic process rather than a measurable achievement. Each gratitude entry contributes to the growth of one plant, which evolves through predefined stages over time.

This metaphor avoids pressure and competition and encourages mindful, low-threshold reflection. The plant acts as a visual companion rather than a goal to optimise.

Idea & Motivation

Many existing gratitude apps rely heavily on text, statistics, or streaks, which can feel overwhelming or discouraging. Gratitude Garden was designed to support emotional wellbeing without performance pressure.

By focusing on calm visuals and slow progression, the app encourages users to reflect at their own pace and build a sustainable habit.
